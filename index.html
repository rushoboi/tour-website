Tour Planner

Rashedul Islam

Introducing Rashedul Islam(Rusho), a skilled tour planner and Dhaka University THM Department graduate of 2022. With a deep passion for travel and extensive industry knowledge, Rusho creates exceptional experiences. Their personalized itineraries cater to clients' preferences, ensuring unique and unforgettable journeys. Rusho possesses destination expertise, from cultural heritage to hidden gems, enabling them to curate diverse trips. Meticulous in managing logistics, they flawlessly execute bookings, transportation, and activities. Exceptional communication skills allow Rusho to understand clients' desires and provide tailored recommendations. They prioritize sustainability, promoting responsible travel and supporting local communities. With professionalism and enthusiasm, Rusho goes above and beyond to exceed expectations. A commitment to seamless experiences, eco-friendly options, and ethical accommodations sets them apart. Rusho combines their passion, knowledge, and dedication to craft extraordinary travel memories. Ready to guide clients on unforgettable journeys, they make dreams come true, one trip at a time.
